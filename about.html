また、他者との違い／共通点を理解し、相手を思いやる気持ちや客観性を、娯楽として楽しみながら養うこと。

Understanding the character of yourself and facing others' various thoughts.
In addition, enjoy developing feelings of kindness towards other people and objectivity while understanding the differences and similarities between others as entertainment.

はじめに Introduction

多種多様な性格があると言葉にすることは簡単ですが、その事実を実感し、受け入れることは容易ではありません。

誰にでも出来る簡単な方法「二択の質問」を基に、誰もが自由に個性を表すことができ、それらを一覧できる場所があれば、「みんな違ってみんないい」っていうことを強く実感できるのでは？という発想から ichoose は始動しました。

It is easy to talk about how characters are diverse, but on the other hand, it is not easy to face it and truly accept it.

We started ichoose based on the idea of realizing powerfully "everyone is different and it’s nice", if everyone is able to express their characters and listed them based on “two choices questions” which is the easy method that everyone can do.
